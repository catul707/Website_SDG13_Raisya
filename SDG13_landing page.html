About

Climate Action Hackathon invites students, researchers and developers to co-create prototypes and datasets that tackle climate-related risks. Expected outcomes include working prototypes, open datasets and technical documentation suitable for pilot trials.

  • Purpose: Rapid prototyping for climate adaptation and mitigation.
  • Goals: Produce at least 5 prototypes and 3 open datasets.
  • Audience: Students, mentors, NGO, and industry partners.

SDG 13 - Climate Action

This event focuses on solutions that reduce climate risks through early warning systems, green infrastructure planning, community resilience apps and low-cost mitigation technologies.

Teams are encouraged to explain how their solution reduces greenhouse gas emissions, increases resilience, or improves climate-related decisions making.

Event Schedule
